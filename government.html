Langkawi Government

Governance & Vision for a Sustainable Island

Leadership and Development

The Langkawi Development Authority (LADA) plays a vital role in administering the island's tourism, infrastructure, and economic growth in line with Malaysia’s Vision 2030.

Departments

  • LADA: Planning, tourism, and investment
  • Municipal Council: Waste, roads, and public health
  • Environmental Unit: Sustainability and conservation
  • Community Relations: Engagement and outreach

Vision

Langkawi aims to become a smart and green island with world-class services by the year 2030, emphasizing eco-tourism and digital innovation.

Langkawi Municipal Council

The administrative authority overseeing development, cleanliness, and local services in Langkawi.

YBhg. Dato’ Haslina binti Abdul Hamid

As CEO of Langkawi Development Authority (LADA), she leads strategic initiatives for sustainable tourism, environmental conservation, and island‑wide growth.

Langkawi Governance & Vision

Langkawi is governed through collaboration among agencies like LADA, the Municipal Council, and the Environmental Unit. They guide the island toward sustainability, eco-tourism, and innovation.

  • LADA: Economic and tourism development
  • Municipal Council: Public services, waste, roads
  • Community Unit: Local outreach and engagement
