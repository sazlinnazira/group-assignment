Top Activities in Langkawi

Island Hopping

Visit iconic islands like Dayang Bunting and Beras Basah, famous for stunning views and serene waters.

SkyCab & SkyBridge

Ride the Langkawi cable car and experience breathtaking views from the top of Gunung Mat Cincang.

Mangrove Tour

Explore the rich ecosystem of Kilim Geoforest Park by boat through ancient mangrove rivers.

Snorkeling & Diving

Discover the colourful marine life at Pulau Payar Marine Park with clear turquoise waters.

Night Market

Shop like a local at the pasar malam and enjoy a variety of street food and handmade crafts.
