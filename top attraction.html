Top Attractions

The must‑visit gems of Langkawi Island

Pantai Cenang

Soft white sand, stunning sunsets and a lively strip of cafés & beach bars – the island’s most famous shoreline.

Pantai Beras Basah Island Hopping

Turquoise water, swaying palms and quiet vibes on a postcard‑perfect islet reached by boat tours.

Tasik Dayang Bunting

An emerald lake cradled by jungle‑clad hills. Kayak, swim or just soak in the legend of the Pregnant Maiden.

Langkawi Cable Car & Sky Bridge

Ride one of the world’s steepest gondolas to panoramic peaks, then walk the iconic curved bridge in the clouds.

Kilim Geopark Mangrove Tour

Boat through UNESCO‑listed mangroves, limestone caves and eagle habitats in a 550‑million‑year‑old landscape.

Pulau Payar Marine Park

Crystal‑clear snorkelling with rainbow reefs and friendly reef fish – ideal for first‑timers & seasoned divers.

Maha Tower Langkawi

Soar to a 360° observation deck in Langkawi’s newest landmark. Magical night lights over Kuah Harbour.

Splash Out Water Park

Wave pools, mega slides and ocean‑explorer theming – perfect for a fun‑filled, family‑friendly cool‑down.
